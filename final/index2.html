Information Design and User Experience

  • Full Scale

    • Choosing the topic of Depression, I created an infographic that visually displays the abstract data found within my collection of 100 select case studies. This project focused on visual organization, ordering, hierarchy of information as well as the filtering of data and visual form.

  • view one view two Full Scale

    • Assigned the location Kenmore Square in Boston, MA, I was tasked with creating a walkable map, using Adobe Illustrator, that visually displays my knowledge of the principles of mapping, collection of spatiotemporal data, information hierarchy, as well as structure and design simplicity for ease of navigation.

  • The original Northbound Amtrak train schedule that was provided and the requirement was to include all the information listed as well as the key to the symbols of the schedule yet redesign the document to make it more user-friendly.

    Full Scale
